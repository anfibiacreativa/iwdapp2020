Google's Women Techmakers program provides visibility, community, and resources for women in technology.

This 2020, we are excited to announce the 5th edition of our annual event: International Women’s Day, on the 21st of March, 2020

Last year we had a full day of speaking breakouts in two tracks (one in Spanish and one in English), with over 150 attendees. This year we will have a lot of more surprises, to continue to highlight and expose women talent, and contribute to reduce the industry gap.

¡Nos complace presentar la quinta edición de nuestro evento anual: International Women’s Day, el día 21 de marzo de 2020!

El año pasado, tuvimos una jornada entera de presentaciones y charlas en dos tracks (español e inglés), para un público de 150 participantes. Este año, ¡muchas más sorpresas! Para seguir impulsando el rol de la mujer, en tecnología.
